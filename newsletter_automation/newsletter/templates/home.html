Hello there!

Welcome to Qxf2's newsletter generator. We developed this application to achieve two goals:

  1. 1. Make newsletter generation quicker
  2. 2. Improve employee reading habits

Links posted on our etc,etc,etc channel on Skype show up on this application. Every article is assigned an article editor who creates a summary for it. On Wednesdays, at 14:00 IST, a group of 3 employees use this application and the links stored here to generate a newsletter on MailChimp. MailChimp sends out the newsletter to our subscribers on Fridays. If you have not already signed up for our newsletter, please sign up here.

Article editors

A good article editor is a key to achieving goals 1 and 2. As an article editor, please ensure you are giving good summaries of the articles that you are reading. Avoid giving lazy one-liners. Your summaries must make the reader want to click on the link. Ensure that the link URLs are clean and do not have unnecessary tracking parameters. Delete articles that are inappropriate for our newsletter. For more information, follow the instructions on our wiki.

Known issues
